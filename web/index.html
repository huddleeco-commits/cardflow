How it works:

  1. Open the link on your phone
  2. Login with your CardFlow account
  3. Point camera at card, tap to capture FRONT
  4. Flip card, tap to capture BACK
  5. Card auto-uploads with sound confirmation
  6. Cards appear here and start identifying automatically
Audio Cues: Beep = Front captured | Double beep = Back captured | Ding = Complete!
